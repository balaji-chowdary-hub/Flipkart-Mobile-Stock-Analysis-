# Flipkart-Mobile-Stock-Analysis-
This Flipkart Mobile Stock Analysis dashboard summarizes 3,114 mobiles across brands. It highlights an average rating of 4.14, average discount of 6.69%, average selling price of 26.44K, and original price of 28.33K. Visuals show brand-wise ratings, discounts, stock count, and category distribution by ratings and discounts.
Project Overview

This project analyzes mobile phone listings from Flipkart to uncover insights on pricing, discounts, ratings, and brand-wise availability. An interactive Power BI dashboard is built to help understand market trends and customer preferences in the smartphone segment.

## Objectives
Analyze mobile stock distribution across brands
Compare average selling price vs original price
Study discount patterns by brand and category
Evaluate customer ratings and rating categories

## Key Insights
Total mobiles analyzed: 3,114
Average rating: 4.14
Average discount: 6.69%
Average selling price: ₹26.44K
Samsung, Apple, and Realme dominate stock availability
Majority of mobiles fall under Good and Excellent rating categories

## Tools & Technologies
Power BI – Data modeling & dashboard creation
Excel / CSV – Data source & preprocessing

## Dashboard Features
Brand-wise average ratings and discounts
Mobile count per brand
Rating category distribution
Discount category analysis
Interactive slicers (Storage, Memory, Colour, Model)

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
Total Mobiles
  Total number of mobile products analyzed (3,114)
Average Rating
  Mean customer rating across all mobile brands (4.14)
Average Discount (%)
  Average discount offered on mobile phones (6.69%)
Average Selling Price (ASP)
  Average current selling price of mobiles (₹26.44K)
Average Original Price
  Average listed price before discount (₹28.33K)
Brand-wise Mobile Count
  Number of mobile models available per brand
Brand-wise Average Rating
  Comparison of customer ratings across different brands
Brand-wise Discount %
  Discount distribution across brands to identify aggressive pricing
Rating Category Distribution
  % of mobiles classified as Excellent, Good, Average, and Below Average
Discount Category Distribution
  Breakdown of mobiles by No, Low, Average, and High discount ranges
